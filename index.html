About SolarWallah

SolarWallah specializes in comprehensive solar PV plant services including IV Curve Testing, EL Testing, Hand Thermography, Barcode Scanning, and Digital Twin Mapping. Our expert team ensures optimal performance and longevity of your solar installations.

Our Services

IV Curve Testing

Analyze and optimize the performance of solar panels through precise IV Curve Testing.

Increase solar efficiency through precision evaluation, issue identification, and optimum performance for maximum energy production.

  • Efficient Evaluation: Swiftly assesses voltage-current relationships, identifying shading, soiling, series resistance, and module mismatch using specialized curve tracers.
  • Troubleshooting and Analytics: IV Testing reduces startup risks, speeds up troubleshooting, and enhances performance modeling with detailed reports and actionable analytics.
  • Quick Evaluation, Less Downtime: Ensures a rapid 15-second per string evaluation, minimizing system downtime and enabling early-stage testing without activating inverters.

EL Testing

Detect potential issues with Electroluminescence Testing to ensure the health of your solar modules.

  • Defect Visualization: Futr OS EL Testing uses Electroluminescence to visualize current distribution in PV cells, detecting microcracks, cell cracks, and soldering defects early.
  • Comprehensive Identification: Covers defects like PID, diode failures, and dead cells for a thorough assessment of solar module integrity.
  • Proactive Assurance: Adopts a proactive approach by addressing microcracks, cell cracks, and cold soldering defects, ensuring structural soundness and long-term performance optimization.

Hand Thermography

Identify and Address Hotspots and Anomalies in Solar Systems.

  • Thermal Imaging: Use advanced thermal imaging to detect and analyze hotspots and anomalies in solar panels.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Enable proactive maintenance by identifying potential issues before they escalate.
  • Optimized Performance: Ensure optimal performance and longevity of solar installations through precise thermal assessments.

Barcode Scanning

Efficient Tracking and Management of Solar Panels.

  • Accurate Tracking: Utilize barcode scanning for precise tracking of solar panel installations.
  • Inventory Management: Streamline inventory management with reliable barcode scanning technology.
  • Data Integration: Integrate barcode data seamlessly with existing systems for efficient operations.

Digital Twin Mapping

Create a Virtual Replica of Your Solar PV Plant for Advanced Monitoring.

  • Virtual Replication: Develop a digital twin of your solar PV plant for real-time monitoring and analysis.
  • Predictive Maintenance: Use digital twins to predict and prevent potential issues, enhancing plant reliability.
  • Performance Optimization: Optimize performance through detailed simulations and data-driven insights.
